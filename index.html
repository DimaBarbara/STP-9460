About the Game

A match-3 puzzle experience built for relaxation and clarity.

Tile Rush: Match 3 Puzzle is a casual puzzle game designed for players of all ages who appreciate calm gameplay and meaningful progression.

Tile Rush: Match 3 Puzzle is a casual puzzle game designed for players of all ages who appreciate calm gameplay and meaningful progression.

Instead of high-stress mechanics or aggressive monetization, the game invites you into a world of simple goals and satisfying rewards.

The core idea is to clear the board by matching three identical tiles, but the beauty is in the subtle strategy behind each move.

We’ve blended visually rich design with responsive controls and relaxing audio to create an environment that’s as therapeutic as it is engaging.

How to play

Simple to Learn. Deeply Satisfying to Master.

/01

Tap tiles to pick them up.

/02

Match 3 of the same kind to clear them.

/03

Clear the board to complete the level.

/04

Use logic and pattern recognition to plan your moves.

/05

No time limits — take all the time you need.
