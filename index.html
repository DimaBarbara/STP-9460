About the Game

A match-3 puzzle experience built for relaxation and clarity.

Tile Rush: Match 3 Puzzle is a casual puzzle game designed for players of all ages who appreciate calm gameplay and meaningful progression.

Tile Rush: Match 3 Puzzle is a casual puzzle game designed for players of all ages who appreciate calm gameplay and meaningful progression.

Instead of high-stress mechanics or aggressive monetization, the game invites you into a world of simple goals and satisfying rewards.

The core idea is to clear the board by matching three identical tiles, but the beauty is in the subtle strategy behind each move.

We’ve blended visually rich design with responsive controls and relaxing audio to create an environment that’s as therapeutic as it is engaging.

How to play

Simple to Learn. Deeply Satisfying to Master.

/01

Tap tiles to pick them up.

/02

Match 3 of the same kind to clear them.

/03

Clear the board to complete the level.

/04

Use logic and pattern recognition to plan your moves.

/05

No time limits — take all the time you need.

Levels

From easy beginnings to satisfying challenges.

Tile Rush features a thoughtfully designed level system that gradually increases in complexity while keeping gameplay enjoyable.

Early levels introduce you to core mechanics, helping you understand the matching system and how different tiles interact. As you progress, new patterns, obstacles, and strategic opportunities emerge.

With over 500+ handcrafted levels (and more added regularly), you’ll encounter evolving challenges like layered tiles, limited moves, and bonus objectives.

Each new set of levels is set in a different visual theme, offering not just variety in gameplay but also aesthetic freshness.

Whether you're here for a light distraction or a serious brain workout, the level progression keeps you moving forward with satisfaction.

Zen Mode

Unwind with every match

Zen Mode in Tile Rush is designed specifically for players who want a stress-free, calming experience.

There are no time limits, no life counters, and no pressure—just you, the tiles, and a peaceful soundtrack.

This mode is perfect for winding down before bed, staying focused during breaks, or simply enjoying a mindful puzzle session without distraction.

The design focuses on visual softness, smooth animations, and ambient audio to help you fully relax while playing.

Whether you're meditating through matches or escaping the noise of daily life, Zen Mode offers a pure and rejuvenating way to enjoy the game at your own rhythm.

It's not just a mode—it’s a digital sanctuary.
