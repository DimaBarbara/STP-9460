Is Tile Rush completely free to play?

Yes, Tile Rush is entirely free to download and play. All levels can be accessed without any payment. While there are optional in-game purchases available (such as boosters or visual themes), none of them are necessary to enjoy the full experience. We’ve carefully designed the game to ensure that progress is possible for all players without ever needing to spend money. Our goal is to provide a relaxing puzzle journey without any paywalls. Players who want to support us can do so through these optional extras.

Do I need an internet connection to play Tile Rush?

No, you do not need an internet connection to enjoy the game. Tile Rush is fully playable offline, making it ideal for travel, commutes, or relaxing moments without Wi-Fi or mobile data. All features, including boosters and Zen Mode, are accessible in offline mode. The game only requires an internet connection if you're updating it or making an optional purchase. This allows you to enjoy uninterrupted gameplay wherever you are. It’s truly a game that travels with you.

What makes Tile Rush different from other match-3 puzzle games?

Tile Rush stands out for its relaxing, no-pressure approach. Unlike many other puzzle games, there are no timers, no intrusive ads, and no lives system that forces you to wait or pay. The pace is entirely up to the player. Every level is designed to be both calming and intellectually satisfying. In addition, our Zen Mode offers an endless play option with no objectives—just peaceful matching. Combined with high-quality visuals and intuitive gameplay, it’s the perfect escape from fast-paced mobile games.

How do boosters work in the game?

Boosters are tools you can use to help clear difficult levels. They include Hint Spark, Tile Swap, and Clear Set, each serving a different purpose. You can earn boosters by completing certain levels, finding extra matches, or through optional purchases. They are not essential for completing levels, but they’re helpful for when you feel stuck or want a bit of an edge. We made sure boosters are balanced so that gameplay remains fair and rewarding. Using them is completely optional and up to you.

Are new levels added regularly?

Yes, we continuously work on expanding the game. With each major update, we introduce new levels, puzzles, and sometimes even special themed events. We also listen to community feedback to improve level design and adjust difficulty where needed. These updates are rolled out automatically if you’re connected to the internet. You’ll be notified when new content becomes available. Our development roadmap includes seasonal tilesets and monthly puzzle packs to keep the game feeling fresh.

What is Zen Mode and how is it different from regular gameplay?

Zen Mode is a special game mode where there are no goals or level restrictions. You simply match tiles in an endless loop designed for relaxation. There’s no pressure to complete objectives or earn stars—just pure gameplay flow. It’s perfect for winding down after a long day or taking a mindful break. Many players use it for stress relief or background play while listening to music or podcasts. You can switch to Zen Mode from the main menu anytime.

How difficult is the game? Is it suitable for all ages?

Tile Rush is designed to be accessible and enjoyable for players of all ages. The early levels are simple enough for beginners and children, while later stages become more strategic and complex. There are no time limits, which removes unnecessary stress and allows players to learn at their own pace. The interface is intuitive and easy to navigate, making it suitable for younger players. At the same time, puzzle enthusiasts will find enough depth in the later levels to stay engaged and challenged.

Can I sync my progress across devices?

Currently, Tile Rush does not offer cross-device syncing. Your progress is saved locally on the device where you play. However, we are working on implementing a cloud-save feature in a future update, which will allow you to continue playing seamlessly on other devices. In the meantime, we recommend backing up your device regularly to avoid any accidental data loss. Once cloud sync becomes available, you’ll receive a notification with setup instructions. We know this is important and appreciate your patience.

Are there any ads in the game?

No, Tile Rush is an ad-free experience by default. We believe that uninterrupted play is key to relaxation, which is why we do not serve video ads or interstitial pop-ups. All our monetization is based on optional in-game purchases only. This means you can enjoy as many levels as you want without being interrupted. If this ever changes, we’ll offer ad-free versions and be transparent about the choices. For now, we’re proud to provide a clean and calm puzzle environment.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We welcome all feedback from our players. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please email us at info@mindrush.pro. Include your device model, game version, and a description of the issue or idea. Our support team typically responds within 24–48 hours. We take all user feedback seriously and use it to improve the game with each update. Many of our new features and level tweaks have come directly from player input.

Is Tile Rush safe for children to play?

Yes, Tile Rush is designed to be family-friendly. The game does not contain any explicit content, microtransactions that encourage excessive spending, or social features requiring parental supervision. It’s a safe, calm, and enriching experience that supports logical thinking and pattern recognition. We do recommend that parents monitor very young children during play to help them understand the controls and avoid accidental purchases. Otherwise, it’s a great game for kids to enjoy independently or with family.
